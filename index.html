Tires

Tire rotation is a crucial part of vehicle maintenance that involves moving each tire to a different position on your vehicle. This helps to ensure that your tires wear evenly and last longer, which can save you money in the long run.
At Smee's Auto Shop, we recommend getting your tires rotated every 5,000 to 7,000 miles, or as specified in your vehicle's owner's manual. Our skilled technicians use the latest equipment and techniques to perform tire rotations quickly and efficiently, ensuring that your vehicle is safe and performing at its best. Trust us to provide the highest quality tire rotation services and keep your vehicle running smoothly for years to come

During a tire rotation, we'll move each tire to a different position on your vehicle to promote even wear. When we balance your tires, we'll adjust the weight distribution to eliminate vibrations and improve handling. Trust us to provide the highest quality tire rotation and balancing services and keep your vehicle running smoothly and safely for years to come.
